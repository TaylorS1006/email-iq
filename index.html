    What this tells us

    The Provider Group segment shows exceptionally high open rates (40–75%) across announcements, webinars, and transactional content, suggesting this audience is highly engaged regardless of content type — but click-through rates tell a more selective story, with clicks concentrated in high-relevance announcements and post-event follow-ups rather than broad promotional sends. The strongest engagement comes from content with clear operational specificity — payer enrollment timelines, credentialing analytics, required field changes — while broader brand or social event invitations lag. The priority action is to double down on operationally-specific messaging and scrutinize whether micro-event sends are reaching the right contacts within this segment.
